AlphaKube

A community for Kubernetes experts who are building the latest platforms.

The Community

This community is for Kubernetes Platform Engineers who are building the next generation of self service cloud platforms using mostly open source solutions and the latest best practices. Like Atlas holding up the world, we are the engineers who make great companies possible.
  • Discuss the latest DevOps practices with leaders.
  • Compare tools with others of have used them at scale.
  • Discover work opportunities with the best of us.
  • Share ideas with architects who have built entire companies from scratch.
  • Meet other Kubernetes Platform experts and find future co-founders.

Motivation

The best part of the DevOps community is DevOps days, the relationships built from those conferences, and the open sharing of the challenges and solutions we're engaged with. AlphaKube strives to mimic that community with a focus on Kubernetes and the tooling needed to build that perfect platform that is always just within reach.

Joining

We're starting out as a Slack channel and LinkedIn group where community members can connect and ask questions knowing others can share their insightful expertise. With enough interest, we'd love to expand the community to a blog share publication, podcast, job board, networking events, and more.
Who should join? If you're a leader who decides on technologies, has worked with the many phases of scale, and are constantly researching the latest Kubernetes tools for security, automation, and monitoring, you'll be a great fit.
